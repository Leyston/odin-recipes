Chicken Milanese

Description

I made this chicken Milanese after I couldn't find a recipe that would work with pasta. It's crispy, quick to make, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C).
  2. Beat eggs with salt and pepper in a shallow dish.
  3. Working with one piece at a time, gently press chicken into flour to coat and shake off any excess.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges.
